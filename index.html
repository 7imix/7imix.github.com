Arduino one-phase rectangle wave generator

This is a simple arduino patch which reads a potentiometer at A0 and A1 to determine the frequency and duty cycle of the generated rectangle wave on pin 9. This version works on the Arduino Uno and outputs one phase.

one-phase-rectangle.ino

Arduino two-phase rectangle wave generator

This is a modification of the one-phase generator which runs on the Arduino Mega 2560, reads potentiometers on A0 and A1 to determine the frequency and duty cycle of the generated phases on pins 5 and 11. The two phases have identical frequency and duty cycle but are 180 degrees out of phase.
